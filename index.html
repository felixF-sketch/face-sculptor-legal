Privacy Policy — Face Sculptor

Published by LEFORT Technologies · Effective 20 April 2026 · Last updated 20 April 2026

Short version: Face Sculptor runs entirely on your phone. We do not collect, store, upload, or share your photos, your face data, or any information about how you use the app. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking.

1. Who we are

Face Sculptor is an Android application published by LEFORT Technologies ("we", "us", "our"). You can contact us at leforttech@gmail.com for any privacy question, concern, or request.

2. What Face Sculptor does

Face Sculptor is a visualization tool that lets you upload a photo of a face and adjust individual facial features (eyes, nose, jaw, brow, and others) using sliders. All of this happens inside the app on your device. The app is intended for personal visualization and is not a medical device or a substitute for professional consultation.

3. What information we collect

We do not collect any personal information. To be specific:

  • We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or account information, because the app has no sign-in or account system.
  • We do not collect or receive the photos you upload into the app.
  • We do not collect or receive the facial-landmark data that the app detects from your photos.
  • We do not collect device identifiers, advertising IDs, or location data.
  • We do not use analytics SDKs, crash-reporting SDKs, or advertising SDKs.

4. How your photos are handled

When you upload a photo into Face Sculptor, the photo is loaded into the app's memory on your device. Face detection and feature adjustment are performed locally in the app using a machine-learning library (face-api.js) that runs on your device. Your photos and any data derived from them (such as facial-landmark coordinates) are never uploaded to our servers or to any third party.

If you choose to save an edited image using the "Save" function, the image is written to your device's own photo gallery through standard Android storage APIs. Nothing leaves your device as a result of that action.

5. Network activity

Face Sculptor does make one type of network request, and we want to be transparent about it: when you first launch the app, it downloads a set of pre-trained machine-learning model files (approximately 10 MB in total) from a content-delivery server controlled by LEFORT Technologies and hosted on Cloudflare R2. These files are read-only weights that the app needs in order to detect facial landmarks on your device. The download consists of standard HTTPS GET requests. No information about you, your photos, or your usage is sent as part of, or alongside, these requests. Once the files are downloaded, they are cached locally and the app may not need to contact the network again on subsequent launches.

Beyond this initial model download, Face Sculptor does not make any outbound network calls.

6. Permissions the app requests

Face Sculptor requests only the minimum Android permissions required to function:

  • Photo / media access — so you can select a photo from your gallery to load into the app.
  • Camera — if and only if you choose to take a new photo directly from within the app.
  • Write to gallery / photos — if and only if you choose to save an edited image back to your device.

These permissions are used exclusively for the purposes described above and are not used to collect, transmit, or share any data.

7. Children

Face Sculptor is intended for users who are 18 years of age or older. The app is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children, because the app does not collect information at all. If you are under 18, please do not use Face Sculptor.

8. Third-party services

Face Sculptor uses the open-source face-api.js library (MIT-licensed) locally on your device to detect facial landmarks. The library runs entirely inside the app on your device and does not transmit any data to its original authors or to any third party. Model weights are served from our own Cloudflare R2 bucket as described in Section 5.

Face Sculptor does not use Firebase, Google Analytics, Google AdMob, Meta / Facebook SDKs, TikTok SDKs, Sentry, Crashlytics, Mixpanel, Amplitude, PostHog, or any similar analytics, advertising, or tracking service.

9. Your rights

Because we do not collect or store any personal information about you, we have no personal data to access, correct, export, or delete. You can remove Face Sculptor from your device at any time through the standard Android uninstall process, which also removes any locally cached model files and any in-app state associated with the app.

10. Changes to this policy

If we change how Face Sculptor handles data in a future version — for example, if a future release introduces optional analytics, user accounts, or cloud features — we will update this policy before that version is released, note the change in the release notes, and update the "Last updated" date at the top of this document. Where the change is material, we will also update the Google Play Data Safety section for the app. Your continued use of the app after such changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
